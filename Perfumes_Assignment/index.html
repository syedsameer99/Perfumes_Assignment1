About The Paarees Parfumes:


For over 25 years we have been selling the widest range of women's perfumes and men's at affordable prices. We stock the fragrances of nearly 130 brands including Giorgio Armani, Dolice gabbana, Calvin Klein, Dunhil,diesel and Hugo Boss both online and across our network of over 260 nationwide stores. We also stock the Common perfume brands Guuci, Addidas & Chanel. Not only do we have the experience of selling perfumes, we also have the expertise to match. Our staff are trained and developed so that they are the most knowledgeable sales advisors within the perfume industry and we can even boast about having the largest number of fragrance graduates nationwide. Online we offer FREE standard delivery, click and collect in 2 hours, or the next working day, and we've even introduced a "Try Me" option on some of our most popular products where you'll receive a free sample, so if you're choosing a new perfume or aftershave you can smell that before you open your order.
